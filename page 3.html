Website Design

This web design, created using Adobe Illustrator, embodies a unique blend of modern aesthetics and user-centric functionality. The layout adheres to a vibrant, monochromatic color scheme, with a dynamic yellow that commands attention and guides the user through the different sections seamlessly. The choice of a superhero-themed mascot in a geometrical, origami-inspired art style serves as a striking focal point, symbolizing strength and creativity, which are key attributes I aim to communicate as the designer.

The navigation is intentionally simplified, with clean, rounded rectangles that stand out against the energetic background, ensuring a user-friendly experience. Typography is chosen for legibility and impact, with bold headings that make key information immediately accessible. The “About Me” and “Skills” sections are presented in a conversational tone, fostering a personal connection with the audience.

In the “Resume” portion, the timeline of employment history is organized in a manner that highlights professional progression, while the “Skills” section is visually categorized for quick comprehension. The inclusion of personal details and a photograph adds a human touch, making the interface feel inviting and relatable.

The design principles here focus on clarity, engagement, and a narrative structure that weaves the creator’s journey into each segment. By using Illustrator, I was able to craft vector graphics that retain their crispness across devices, ensuring a consistent visual experience. This web design is not just about aesthetic pleasure but also about creating an intuitive pathway for users to understand and interact with the content effectively.

Mcdonald Poster

As the designer behind this poster, my goal was to create a visually captivating experience that communicates the indulgence of the McCrispy meal. Utilizing Adobe Illustrator, I employed a stark contrast between the rich darkness of the background and the vibrant warmth emanating from the product itself. This contrast not only draws the eye directly to the food but also enhances the textures and natural sheen, suggesting a freshness that is both inviting and alluring. The strategic lighting creates an almost celestial aura around the items, positioning them as the heroes of the narrative. The reflection on the glossy surface below adds depth, suggesting a world beyond the frame where the McCrispy is revered.

The text "Love at First Bite" is set in a sophisticated serif typeface, conveying a timeless elegance that complements the modernity of the visual elements. It’s a statement that resonates with the emotional journey of tasting something delightful for the first time. Meanwhile, the golden arches of the McDonald's logo serve as the stamp of trust and quality, seamlessly integrating into the composition without overpowering the main attraction. The poster's overall design is a careful balance between minimalism and expressiveness, aiming to create a memorable impression that tempts the viewer into a sensory experience that can only be completed by visiting McDonald's and taking that first, unforgettable bite.

Castle on Ice

In this digital composition, I envisioned a castle that defies natural landscapes, bringing a touch of fantastical architecture to the stark beauty of a polar realm. Using Photoshop, I meticulously blended a majestic castle onto a floating iceberg, ensuring that the reflections and shadows on the ice mirrored the grandeur of the structure. The castle, with its turrets reaching towards the sky, making it more realistic, while the icy waters and the distant glaciers remind us of the planet's raw, untouched corners. The blend of warm stone against the cool blues of the ice creates a visual dialogue between the man-made and the natural.

The title "CASTLE ON ICE" floats above in a whimsical script, intentionally invoking a sense of wonder and enchantment. As the creator, I aimed to strike a balance between realism and fantasy. The text, appearing almost as a whisper against the vastness of the scene, invites the audience to weave their own stories about this icy fortress. Each element was carefully adjusted for color, tone, and perspective to create an image that invites curiosity and evokes the serene, yet powerful energy of an otherworldly escape.

Website Wireframe

As the creator of this website wireframe designed in Adobe XD, my focus was on creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface for an e-commerce platform specializing in health and nutrition products. The wireframe presents a clean and structured layout with a clear hierarchy, guiding the user's eye through featured products, testimonials, and best sellers, ensuring a logical and efficient shopping experience. The color palette is minimal yet striking, with a vibrant green accentuating call-to-action buttons and highlighting sections, which not only adds visual appeal but also serves as a navigational aid.

The use of white space around the products and text boxes makes for an uncluttered and focused user journey, encouraging exploration without overwhelming the senses. Testimonials are strategically placed to build trust and credibility, featuring customer photos for a personal touch. The bottom section provides easily accessible contact information and reinforces customer support, further adding to the user-centric design philosophy. Every element in this wireframe is carefully thought out to optimize the user's path to purchase while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

Health Application Wireframe

As the creator of this app wireframe designed in Adobe XD, my intention was to craft a user interface that promotes a structured and goal-oriented learning experience. This wireframe illustrates a mobile app for TOUCH Community Services, focusing on personal development and tracking progress. The first screen is a motivational dashboard featuring three key actions: "Track your progress," "Start learning," and "Write your goals." Each action is encapsulated in a distinct, image-centric button that invites interaction and represents the activity visually, making it intuitive for users to navigate.

The second screen is part of the "Learning mode," which showcases a gamified approach with a clear, engaging pathway for learning. It starts with an unlocked module, "Learn About Nutrition," and subsequent sections are locked, indicating a progressive learning journey that encourages completion of each step. A progress indicator at the bottom reinforces the user's advancement and achievements. The use of red for calls to action and progress tracking against the neutral grey of locked sections creates a visual hierarchy that guides the user's journey through the app, making it both user-friendly and visually coherent.

Mobile Portfolio Wireframe

As the designer of this mobile portfolio wireframe created in Adobe XD, my vision was to produce a sleek, personal branding experience that would immediately engage users upon arrival. The design employs a striking black background that allows the vibrant red elements to pop, ensuring that the user's attention is drawn to key features and navigation prompts. The top of the screen is dominated by a bold, stylized logo that serves as a distinct personal branding element, reflecting a dynamic and creative personality.

Below the logo, the welcome screen is straightforward and inviting, with a clear, friendly greeting in a modern sans-serif font. This not only sets a conversational tone but also informs users about the content and the personal nature of the portfolio. Large 'Contact Me' and 'See More' call-to-action buttons are prominently displayed, providing clear pathways for users to interact and explore further. This wireframe emphasizes a user-centric approach to design, prioritizing navigational clarity and visual impact to create a memorable personal portfolio experience on mobile devices.
